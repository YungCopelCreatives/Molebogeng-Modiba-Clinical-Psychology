Professional Services

Scope of Services

We provide clinical psychology services including:

  • Individual psychotherapy and counseling
  • Psychological assessments and evaluations
  • Couples and family therapy
  • Group therapy and workshops
  • Online therapy sessions
Professional Standards

All services are provided in accordance with:

  • Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) guidelines
  • Evidence-based clinical practices
  • Ethical standards for clinical psychologists
  • Continuing professional development requirements

Client Responsibilities

Attendance and Punctuality
  • Arrive on time for scheduled appointments
  • Provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations
  • Attend sessions regularly as agreed upon
  • Complete agreed-upon homework or exercises
Communication and Cooperation
  • Communicate openly and honestly during sessions
  • Actively participate in the therapeutic process
  • Inform the therapist of any changes in circumstances
  • Respect the therapeutic relationship boundaries
Payment and Billing
  • Pay for services as agreed upon
  • Provide accurate insurance or payment information
  • Understand that missed appointments may incur charges
  • Discuss any financial concerns with the therapist

Confidentiality and Privacy

Client Information Protection
  • All client information is kept strictly confidential
  • Information is only shared with explicit written consent
  • Electronic records are securely stored and encrypted
  • Physical records are stored in locked, secure facilities
Exceptions to Confidentiality

Confidentiality may be breached in the following situations:

  • Imminent risk of harm to self or others
  • Suspected child abuse or elder abuse
  • Court orders or legal requirements
  • Consultation with other healthcare professionals (with consent)

Appointment Policies

Scheduling
  • Appointments are scheduled during regular business hours
  • Emergency sessions may be available outside regular hours
  • Online sessions require stable internet connection
  • In-person sessions are held at the Garsfontein practice
Cancellation Policy
  • 24-hour notice required for cancellations
  • Late cancellations or no-shows may incur full session fees
  • Emergency cancellations will be considered on a case-by-case basis

Treatment Process

Therapeutic Approach
  • Treatment plans are developed collaboratively
  • Progress is regularly assessed and discussed
  • Referrals to other specialists may be recommended when appropriate
  • Treatment may be terminated by either party with appropriate notice
Client Rights
  • Right to informed consent and clear explanations
  • Right to ask questions about treatment
  • Right to refuse treatment or seek second opinions
  • Right to access your own records

Limitation of Liability

Professional Services Disclaimer
  • Services are provided to the best of professional ability
  • Outcomes cannot be guaranteed as they depend on many factors
  • Therapy requires active client participation for success
  • Referrals to other professionals may be necessary for comprehensive care
Emergency Situations

In case of mental health emergencies:

  • Call emergency services: 10111
  • Go to nearest emergency room
  • Contact crisis intervention services
