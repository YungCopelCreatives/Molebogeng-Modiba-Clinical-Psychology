Lead Clinical Psychologist

Steven Ntsime Ntlatleng

Click to expand profile info

I am a clinical psychologist based in Tshwane. I hold a Master of science degree in Clinical psychology from Sefako Makgatho University and I am registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa. I am also currently employed as a full-time Clinical Psychologist at 1 Military Hospital.

I hold the notion that “therapy is a dress rehearsal to life” – Irvin Yalom. Psychotherapy is therefore a great preparatory and supportive space to address whatever life challenges a person may face such as; personal, relationship/marital difficulties, work related stressors, parenting issues, school related issues etc. Therefore, I engage clients in systematic and humanistic orientations to promote self-awareness, growth and interpersonal understanding. Our therapeutic point of departure is embedded in the fact that mental health difficulties are mostly relational.
