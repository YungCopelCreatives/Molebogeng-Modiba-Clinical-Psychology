About MCM Psych Space

A Safe Space for Your Healing Journey

Welcome to MCM Psych Space. We are Steven Ntsime Ntlatleng and Molebogeng Modiba, a team of dedicated clinical psychologists based in Tshwane. We believe that "therapy is a dress rehearsal to life" (Irvin Yalom) and are here to provide a supportive, non-judgmental space for your healing journey. Our approaches are tailored to your unique needs, drawing from psychodynamic, CBT, and humanistic orientations.

Psychodynamic and CBT therapeutic approaches
